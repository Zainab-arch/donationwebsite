Support Shumaila's Education Journey

Shumaila is a 7-year-old girl from the rural region of Tharparkar, located in the southern part of Pakistan. Born into a family that struggles to make ends meet, Shumaila's parents are unable to afford her education. Despite their hardships, they are determined to see their daughter succeed.

Shumaila is an intelligent and eager learner. Every day, she watches other children go to school, dreaming of the day she can join them. She is passionate about learning and has a particular interest in science, dreaming of one day becoming a doctor. However, her parents, who work hard to provide for the family, earn only enough to cover basic needs like food and clothing

Living in Tharparkar, a region known for its severe poverty and lack of resources, Shumaila faces numerous challenges. Education is not readily available, and many children, especially girls, are deprived of the opportunity to go to school. Shumaila's family does everything they can to support her, but they know that without external help, her dreams of becoming a doctor will remain out of reach.
