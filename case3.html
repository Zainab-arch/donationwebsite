Support Amna's Fight Against Lymphoma

This incredible 6-year-old has spent her days in the ICU, but now she needs our help. Addie has been diagnosed with stage 3 Hodgkin’s Lymphoma, and she’s facing this fight with extreme grit and grace.

It all started in September when Addie got a week-long fever, chills, night sweats, and aches accompanied by shortness of breath, severe exhaustion, and fatigue. Then a week later it returned, for a week. Then a week after that one it returned, again. And so on, she's lived with intermittent symptoms like this since September. For 3 months she went to multiple doctors and specialists until at the end of November a CT Scan of her chest suggested she had Lymphoma - we are grateful that an Intensive Care Physician at her hospital, who she was talking to during her shift, told her to get a scan of her chest

The CT Scan diagnosis was confirmed through a biopsy. Then a PET Scan showed it to be Stage 3 Hodgkins Lymphoma. For the past 3 months, she's battled multiple instances of fevers, chills, aches, and fatigue without a clear answer of what was going on with her body. We are now hopeful because we have a diagnosis, a plan of action going forward, and a great team of doctors helping us through this
