How to Request a Donation

We are here to support you in times of need. Follow the steps below to request a donation:

Step 1: Prepare Your Information

  • Ensure you have your personal details ready (e.g., full name, email, city).
  • Identify the reason for your request (e.g., healthcare, education, disaster relief).
  • Prepare any relevant documents, such as your CNIC or proof of need.

Step 2: Fill Out the Request Form

Go to the Donation Form and fill in all the required fields. Make sure to:

  • Provide accurate contact information so we can reach you.
  • Clearly explain your need for the donation.
  • Upload all necessary documents to support your request.

Step 3: Submit Your Request

Once you've completed the form, review your details and click Submit. Our team will review your request and get back to you as soon as possible through an email.

What Documents Do You Need?

Make sure to upload the following:

  • Picture of your CNIC
  • Relevant Pictures
  • Bank Statements (if available)
  • Medical Reports
  • Fee vouchers/bills

Need Assistance?

If you have any questions or need help filling out the form, please contact us at support@donations.com.
